# Privacy Policy — Poker Tallies and Rowies

**Last updated:** April 14, 2026

Poker Tallies and Rowies does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any third parties.

The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

If you contact the developer for support, any information you choose to send (for example, your email address) is used only to respond to you and is not sold or shared for marketing purposes.

For questions about this policy, use the support contact listed on the app’s App Store listing.
